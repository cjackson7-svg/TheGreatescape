The Great Escape

Join Us

Step into a world where nothing is as it seems. At The Great Escape Killeen, you and your team will be locked inside a themed room filled with mysterious clues, hidden codes, and mind-bending puzzles.

Whether you're chasing ancient treasure, escaping a haunted cabin, or solving a high-stakes mystery, every minute brings new twists and turns. The thrill of discovery and teamwork will keep your heart racing until the final second.

Rooms

Each escape room tells a unique story — from eerie cabins deep in the woods to secret laboratories and forgotten prisons. Every room is built with cinematic detail, featuring props, lighting, and sound effects that make the experience come alive.
